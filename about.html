ABOUT US

Socio-Path is a social organization working for the people who needs financial support. We provide assistance to the families who lost their people lives in pandemic.
We have been working since 2015 and have helped more than 500+ families across India in situations like Floods, Tsunamis, Earthquakes, etc natural calamities.
Now, we are working towards pandemic hit people by providing a financial aid. Your Smallest contribution to Socio-Path will be encouraging and very helpful for everyone.
Our tagline says it all, "Helping the Needy".
